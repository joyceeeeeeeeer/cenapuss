Commercial & OEM

What is the MOQ for OEM smart retail freezers?

MOQ depends on model, customization depth, and destination requirements. Share target quantity and specs for a proposal.

Do you support ODM with branding?

Yes—appearance, graphics, and certain structural options are common OEM/ODM scopes.

Which certifications do you hold?

CCC, ISO9001, and ISO14001. Market-specific certifications should be discussed as a project topic.

Product & power

What voltage/frequency are supported?

220V/50Hz and 110V/60Hz configurations are supported on standard offerings.

What is the operating temperature range?

Standard range is 0–10°C for the listed smart retail freezer models.

How do I choose between models?

Start from traffic, SKU sizes, footprint, and replenishment plan—see Solutions and product pages.
