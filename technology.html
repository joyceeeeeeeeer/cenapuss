Product technology

Technology for smart retail freezers

Cenapus cabinets combine refrigeration reliability with transaction and connectivity features expected in modern unmanned retail. This page summarizes capability areas; exact interfaces are confirmed per project.

Recognition

AI dynamic vision recognition supports product-level identification workflows in unattended retail.

  • Designed for typical packaged cold SKUs
  • Deployment tuning depends on SKU mix and lighting

Payment

Multiple payment modalities help cover domestic and cross-border operator requirements.

  • QR (WeChat Pay / Alipay)
  • NFC
  • Facial recognition payment (where applicable)
  • POS card terminal support

Connectivity

4G full-network module supports distributed deployments where fixed ethernet is not available.

  • Suitable for multi-point operators
  • Remote monitoring aligned with your backend workflow

Operations

Operational features focus on replenishment efficiency and basic sales visibility—details vary by software stack.

  • Remote status and event reporting (project-dependent)
  • Support for operator SOPs and training materials
